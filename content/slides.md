##### For full screen mode:
1. Click the "Menu" or three vertical dots button.
2. In the menu click "Enter Full Screen". 

<br />

The keyboard shortcut for full screen mode is **Ctrl + Shift + F**. 

Press **Esc** to exit full screen mode.
