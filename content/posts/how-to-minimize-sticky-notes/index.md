<h1 id="1">How to Minimize a Sticky Note With Click</h1>

* Step 1: First [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-notes-list/) the Notes List and [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-a-sticky-note/) a sticky note. Click anywhere inside the Notes List. The Sticky note minimizes and remains behind the Notes List. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking in the Notes List in the background, with the open sticky note in the foreground. The second screenshot displays the Notes List as the sticky note minimizes.](blogpptclickinnoteslisttominnote.png "Click in the Notes List")</div>

<h1 id="2">How to Minimize a Sticky Note From the Notes List</h1>

* Step 1: [Open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-notes-list/) the Notes List and [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-a-sticky-note/) a sticky note. Click inside the sticky note and type any text. At the top of the sticky note, click, hold, drag, and release to move the sticky note anywhere outside the Notes List.<div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor moving the edited sticky note to the left of the Notes List.](blogeditednotebeforeminedit.png "Move the edited sticky note out of the Notes List")</div>
* Step 2: Click the "Menu" or "..." button on the far right side of a sticky note in the Notes List. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "..." button on the sticky note in the Notes List.](blognoteslistclick3dotsedit.png "Click the '...' ")</div>
* Step 3: In the menu that opens, click "Close note". The sticky note minimizes and does not appears on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Close note" option in the menu.](blogpptnoteslist3dotsminimized.png "Click 'Close note' ")</div>

<h1 id="3">How to Minimize a Sticky Note From the Notes List With Right Click</h1>

* Step 1: [Open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-notes-list/) the Notes List and [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-a-sticky-note/) a sticky note. Click inside the sticky note and type any text. At the top of the sticky note, click, hold, drag, and release to move the sticky note anywhere outside the Notes List.<div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor moving the edited sticky note to the left of the Notes List.](blogeditednotebeforeminedit.png "Move the edited sticky note out of the Notes List")</div>
* Step 2: Right click a sticky note in the Notes List. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor right clicking the sticky note in the Notes List.](blognoteslistrightclickminedit.png "Right click the sticky note in the Notes List")</div>
* Step 3: In the menu that opens, click "Close note". The sticky note minimizes and does not appear on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Close note" option in the menu.](blogpptnoteslistrightclickmin.png "Click 'Close note' ")</div>

<h1 id="4">How to Minimize a Sticky Note With Menu</h1>

* Step 1: First [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-sticky-notes/) the Sticky Notes app. Go down to the taskbar and hover the mouse over the Sticky Notes app icon. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Close note" option in the menu.](bloghoveroverappiconedit.png "Click 'Close note' ")</div>
* Step 2: Two small windows appear; one is the Notes List and the other is the sticky note. Right click the small sticky note window.<div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor right clicking the small sticky window.](blogrightclickstickynote.png "Right click the small sticky note window")</div> 
* Step 3: In the Menu that opens, click "Minimize". The sticky note minimizes and does not display on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Minimize" option and the sticky note minimizing.](blogminimizestickynote.png "Click 'Minimize' ")</div> 

<h1 id="5">How to Minimize the Notes List With Click</h1>

* Step 1: First open any other window or app, such as the Microsoft Store. Then [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-notes-list/) the Notes List. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the open Microsoft Store window in the background and the open Notes List in the foreground.](blogclickoutofnoteslisttominimize1edit.png "Open another app and the Notes List")</div>
* Step 2: Click anywhere outside of the Notes List. The Notes List window minimizes and does not display on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the open Microsoft Store window in the background and the open Notes List in the foreground.](blogpptclickoutofnoteslisttominimize.png "Open another app and the Notes List")</div>

<h1 id="6">How to Minimize the Notes List With Menu</h1>

* Step 1: First [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-sticky-notes/) the Sticky Notes app. Go down to the taskbar and hover the mouse over the Sticky Notes app icon. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor hovering over the Sticky Notes app icon on the taaskbar](bloghoveroverappiconedit.png "Hover over the app icon")</div>
* Step 2: Two small windows appear; one is the Notes List and the other is the sticky note. Right click the small Notes List window.<div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor right clicking the small Notes List window.](blogrightclicknoteslist.png "Right click the small sticky note window")</div>
* Step 3: In the Menu that opens, click "Minimize". The Notes List minimizes and does not display on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Minimize" option and the Notes List window minimizing.](blogminimizenoteslist.png "Click 'Minimize' ")</div> 

<h1 id="7">How to Minimize the Notes List With Right Click</h1>

* Step 1: [Open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-sticky-notes/) the Sticky Notes app. Go down to the taskbar and right click the Sticky Notes app icon. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor right clicking the Sticky Notes app icon on the taskbar.](blogrightclickappiconedit.png "Right click the app icon")</div>
* Step 2: In the menu that opens, click "Hide all notes". Both the Notes List and all sticky notes minimize and do not display on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Hide all notes" option in the menu.](blogppthideallnotesrightclick.png "Click 'Hide all notes' ")</div>

<h1 id="8">How to Minimize the Notes List With Start</h1>

* Step 1: First [open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-sticky-notes/) the Sticky Notes app. Go down to the taskbar and click Start (four blue squares). <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the Start (four blue squares) button on the taskbar at the bottom of the screen.](blogstartbuttonedit.png "Click the Start button")</div>
* Step 2: In the Start window that opens, click the top search bar and type "sticky notes". <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the search bar at the top of the Start window.](blogsearchbaredit.png "Search for 'sticky notes' ")</div>
* Step 3: On the search results screen, click "Hide all notes". Both the Notes List and all sticky notes minimize and do not display on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the cursor clicking the "Hide all notes" option in the search results.](blogppthideallnotesstart.png "Click 'Hide all notes' ")</div>

<h1 id="9">How to Minimize the Notes List With Keyboard Shortcut</h1>

* Step 1: [Open](https://qhtutorials.github.io/posts/how-to-open-sticky-notes/) the Sticky Notes app. On the keyboard, press **Ctrl + O**. Both the Notes List and all sticky notes minimize and do not display on the computer screen. <div class="stepimage">![A screenshot of the Notes List and sticky notes, with a graphic of the keyboard keys "Ctrl + O". The second screenshot displays the empty Desktop as all windows minimize.](blogpptctrlo.png "Press Ctrl + O")</div>
