How to Add a City With Click

  • Step 1: First switch to the World Clock mode. Click the “+” or “Add a new city” button.
  • Step 2: In the “Add new location” window that opens, type any text, then click the suggested city.
  • Step 3: Click the “+ Add” button.
  • The World Clock displays the new city.

How to Add a City With Keyboard Shortcut

  • Step 1: Switch to the World Clock mode. On the keyboard press Ctrl + N.
  • Step 2: In the “Add new location” window that opens, type any text, then click the suggested city.
  • Step 3: Click the “+ Add” button.
  • The World Clock displays the new city.

How to Add a City With Keyboard

  • Step 1: First switch the World Clock mode. On the keyboard press Alt + A.
  • Step 2: In the “Add new location” window that opens, type any text, then click the suggested city.
  • Step 3: Click the “+ Add” button.
  • The World Clock displays the new city.

How to Clear the "Add New Location" Window With Click

  • Step 1: Add a new city. Click the “X” button to the right of the search bar.

How to Clear the "Add New Location" Window With Backspace

  • Step 1: First add a new city. Click the search bar, and on the keyboard press Backspace.

How to Clear the "Add New Location" Window With Delete

  • Step 1: Add a new city. Select all the text in the search bar, then on the keyboard press Delete.

How to Exit the "Add New Location" Window

  • Step 1: First add a new city. Click the “Cancel” button.
  • Alternatively, click anywhere outside the window.

How to Delete a City With Click

  • Step 1: Switch to the World Clock mode. Click the “Edit Clocks” or pencil button.
  • Step 2: On the left side of a city, click the “Delete” or trash can button.
  • Step 3: Click the “Done” or check mark button.
  • The World Clock removes the deleted city.

How to Delete a City With Right Click

  • Step 1: First switch to the World Clock mode. Right click a city.
  • Step 2: In the menu that opens, click “Delete”.
  • The World Clock removes the deleted city.

How to Delete a City With Keyboard Shortcut

  • Step 1: Switch to the World Clock mode. On the keyboard press Alt + E.
  • Step 2: On the left side of a city, click the “Delete” or trash can button.
  • Step 3: On the keyboard press Alt + D.
