How to Undo With Right Click

  • Step 1: First edit a sticky note. Right click anywhere in the note.
  • Step 2: In the menu that opens, click “Undo”. The previous action is reversed.

How to Undo With Keyboard Shortcut

  • Step 1: Edit a sticky note. On the keyboard, press Ctrl + Z. The previous action is reversed.

How to Redo With Right Click

  • Step 1: First undo, then right click anywhere in the sticky note.
  • Step 2: In the menu that opens, click “Redo”. The previous “Undo” is reversed.

How to Redo With Keyboard Shortcut

  • Step 1: Undo in a sticky note. On the keyboard press Ctrl + Y. The previous “Undo” is reversed.
